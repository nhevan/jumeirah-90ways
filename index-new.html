About

Add a different flavour to your holidays when you stay in Jumeirah Hotels & Resorts. Enjoy complimentary daily breakfast and a choice of lunch or dinner in over 60 participating restaurants. Embark on a culinary journey across 6 luxurious hotels and resorts in Dubai, including Burj Al Arab Jumeirah.

Inclusions

  • Dining

    Enjoy a wide range of incredible privileges including daily complimentary breakfast and a unique dine around experience with a choice of lunch or dinner across over 60 participating restaurants located across Dubai including Burj Al Arab Jumeirah’s restaurants.

  • Wild Wadi Waterpark™

    Complimentary unlimited access to Wild Wadi Waterpark™. The ultimate family fun destination offering pure aquatic pleasure, this themed park has over 30 rides and attractions which guarantee a fantastic time for children and adults alike.

  • Beaches

    Complimentary access to exclusive beaches. Enjoy the stunning, two-kilometre private beach next to Madinat Jumeirah and Jumeirah Beach Hotel. If you are staying at the enchanting Jumeirah Zabeel Saray you will also have access to a pristine and exclusive private beach.

  • Internet

    Complimentary Internet. All of our hotels offer complimentary in-room internet access so you can make sure that you are never out of touch and you can share your holiday moments with family and friends.

  • Kids Club

    Complimentary access to Kid’s Clubs. Accessible to guests staying at Dubai hotels, offer a safe, fun and friendly environment for your children to explore and make new friends in closely supervised surroundings.
